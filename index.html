OUR HISTORY

Weather have always been an important factor for us humans. All the way back to the early morning weather decided the daily activities to us humans. But as a difference from them, we can actually check the weather by just clicking on our phone.
Even tho we have hundreds of different weather broadcast online, the it-student Jan Thomas Trinborg decided to create a better and more stylish way to check the weather. On 24. October 2018 this dream became true when he started the company DailyWeather.
